About me

Nikki Kolk, a fashion designer and creative based in Amsterdam.
My work explores the intersection of traditional tailoring and modern expression.

Born in 2005, I’ve been designing and creating garments from a young age. I studied Fashion Tailoring at ROC Amsterdam Zuid, where I developed a strong technical foundation in design, pattern construction and garment creation.

I gained professional experience at Katharina Spitz and House of Denim Foundation, where I expanded my understanding of design processes and garment making. I’m currently part of the House of Denim Incubator Program, where I continue to focus on my practice while developing my own label, Nikki Kolk. I aim to create timeless, expressive pieces that show craftsmanship.
